BRICK CRUSADERS

Brick Crusaders is a strategic medieval fantasy tabletop wargame that utilizes LEGO Minifigures as the primary models for gameplay.

This solo-designed project, crafted by Natalie, is in its early stages and constantly evolving. Two factions are currently in development: Humans and Undead (faction names pending).

Expect ongoing updates to ensure gameplay is balanced, engaging, and ever-expanding.
